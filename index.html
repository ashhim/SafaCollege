About Safa College

Safa Arts and Science College is a private, self-financing institute established in 2009, located in Malappuram, Kerala. The college offers 14 specialized courses in streams including Science, IT & Software, Business & Management Studies, Accounting & Commerce, and Mass Communication & Media.

The courses offered include B.Sc., BCA, BCom, BA, BBA and postgraduate programs like MCom and MA. Fees range from INR 51,660 to INR 1,12,500.

BCA (Bachelor of Computer Applications)

BCA is a 3-year undergraduate program focusing on computer science, programming, and IT applications. Safa College offers BCA Honours with an intake capacity of 36 students.

The program covers various programming languages and IT skills, preparing students for careers in software development, system administration, and more.

Fees for BCA fall within the range of INR 51,660 to INR 1,12,500.
